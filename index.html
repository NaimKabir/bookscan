bookscan.app is the spiritual successor to certain benign tools that were used solely to provide descriptive statistics and sentiment analysis on works of prose.

There is a key difference: this application is intentionally spiteful. It was trained specifically on the prose works of every author that jumped onto the internet dogpile that got this tool taken off the internet without any inspection of the intent nor the underlying technology.

Instead of being purely descriptive, this application is generative, and allows anyone to effortlessly imitate the style and content of these authors just by clicking the 'Generate' button below.
